How to delete the account in Uga Dooga's games

If you want to delete your account, please follow these steps.

  1. Open the main menu of the chosen game
  2. Make the screenshot with your ID
  3. Send the email to the support@ugadooga.com with this screenshot and request to delete your account

What will be deleted: the specified username, authentication data, and other profile data. Storage period up to 90 days.
