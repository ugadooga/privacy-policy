How to delete the account in Uga Dooga's games

In this game account is local and it's stored only on your device. If you want to delete your account, please follow these steps.

For Android users:

  1. Go to "Settings" on your Android device
  2. Scroll down and select "Apps"
  3. Find the needed app and select it
  4. Tap on "Storage"
  5. Tap on "Clear Data"
  6. Click "OK" on "All data deletion" warning or "Clear App Data". Attention! All your progress will be lost after clearing data.

For iOS users:

  1. Delete the chosen game and your account will be deleted

What will be deleted: the specified username and other profile data. Account data will be deleted immediately after the instructions are completed.
